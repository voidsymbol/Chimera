The Integer Arena & Spatial Locality

Why didn't 200,000 particles crash the main thread? Because we bypassed the JavaScript garbage collector and leveraged CPU Caching. By writing pixel data to a flat, sequential Uint32Array, we unlocked Spatial Locality.
